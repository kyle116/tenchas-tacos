Tencha's Tacos

Tacos are crisp-fried corn tortillas filled with seasoned ground beef, cheese, lettuce, and sometimes tomato, onion, salsa, sour cream, and avocado or guacamole.

Learn more

Background

After working in the food industry for over two decades, Chef Orten (Tencha) and her husband Jose decided to take their tacos on the road throughout the City of Angels.

Menu

Minimum 50 guests, prices start at $8 per guest. All orders come with choice of three meats, beans, rice, 2 salsas, chopped onion & cilantro, grilled onion, grilled peppers, radishes, and cucumber. Plates and utensils are also provided. All other items on menu come at extra charge, please see stated prices for each on menu below.
